## Algorithm

- Default to the simplest rule.

**Types of rules**
- Even, Odd series
- Difference is common number (Arithemetic progression)
- Multiplication by common number (Geometric progression)
- Prime series
- Square series
- Cube series
- Some Power series
- Fibonacci series
- Factorial series
- Rotate each digit
